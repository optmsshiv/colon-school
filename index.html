Three Ways to Join Us

Flexible admission types designed around every family's needs — from daily commuters to full residential life.

Program 01

Day Scholar

Attends school daily, returns home in the evening

Nursery – Class VIII Morning to Evening All Meals Optional

Perfect for families living nearby. Students enjoy the full Sainik experience — discipline, academics, and co-curriculars — and return home each evening.

Program 02

Day Boarder

Stays on campus through the day and evening, goes home at night

Nursery – Class VIII Meals Included Evening Study

Includes morning to night campus supervision, all meals, and structured evening study sessions — with the comfort of sleeping at home.

Program 03

Full Boarder

Lives on campus 24/7 in a structured Sainik residential environment

Class I – Class VIII Full Board & Lodging NCC & Drill

The complete Sainik experience. Students live, study, eat, and grow together — building camaraderie, discipline, and resilience under 24/7 expert care.

Who We Are

More Than a School — A Movement

Colonel's Sainik Vidyalaya is a CBSE-affiliated school in Madhepura, Bihar — founded with an extraordinary vision: to bring the discipline and values of India's armed forces into mainstream education, accessible to every family.

We are not just another school. We are a sanctuary of discipline, purpose, and academic excellence — where children are not merely taught, but shaped. Every morning drill, every classroom lesson, every evening study session is intentional, structured, and filled with care.

Founded by Dr. Anamika Kumar (W/o Col. Shishir Kumar), the school was built on the belief that when structured military discipline meets compassionate education, extraordinary human beings emerge.
